Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

What age groups do you teach?

We teach students of all ages, from 5 years old to adults. Our curriculum is tailored to match the learning pace and style of each age group, ensuring optimal progress for everyone.

Do I need to bring my own chess set?

No, all chess equipment including boards, pieces, and clocks are provided by the academy. However, students are welcome to bring their own sets if they prefer.

How long does it take to become a good player?

Progress varies by individual, but with consistent practice and attendance, most students see significant improvement within 3-6 months. We focus on building a strong foundation and developing critical thinking skills.

Can I switch between branches?

Yes! Students enrolled at Perfect Chess Academy can attend classes at any of our three branches based on their convenience. Just inform us in advance so we can coordinate with the instructors.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online chess coaching. Our online classes are conducted via video conferencing with the same quality instruction as our physical classes.

What are your fees and payment options?

We offer flexible payment plans including monthly, quarterly, and annual options. Fees vary based on the course level and duration. Contact us for detailed pricing information and available discounts.
