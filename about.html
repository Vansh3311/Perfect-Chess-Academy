Our Approach

Our Story

15 Years of Building Champions

From a humble classroom in 2010 to Bikaner's premier chess institution—our journey has been defined by dedication, innovation, and the success of our students.

2010

The First Move

Founded by FIDE Master Kapil Panwar with 5 students, establishing Rajasthan's premier chess institution.

FIDE Master Kapil Panwar founded Perfect Chess Academy with a vision to make quality chess education accessible in Bikaner...

  • Launched first beginner program
  • Established structured curriculum
  • Built foundation for growth
2012

Growing Recognition

Our students began making waves in regional tournaments. The academy expanded to 50+ students, and we introduced intermediate and advanced training modules. Our reputation for producing disciplined, well-prepared players began to spread across Bikaner.

  • First State-level medal winners
  • Introduced advanced curriculum
  • Student base grew 10x
2015

A Champion Emerges

Breakthrough moment: Our student Arjun clinched the Rajasthan State U-14 Championship title, defeating 120+ competitors. This landmark victory validated our teaching methodology and attracted serious chess talents from across the state. Media coverage put Perfect Chess Academy on the map.

  • State Championship victory
  • Featured in regional press
  • Attracted top young talents
2018

New Headquarters & Expansion

Opened our flagship 3,500 sq ft facility featuring a dedicated tournament hall, analysis room with 20 digital boards, extensive chess library, and comfortable waiting area for parents. Simultaneously launched our second branch to serve Bikaner's growing chess community.

  • State-of-the-art main facility
  • Opened 2nd Bikaner branch
  • Hosted first FIDE-rated event
2020

Digital Transformation

Responding to global changes, we rapidly developed our online learning platform. What began as an emergency pivot became a game-changing innovation, allowing us to continue training students remotely while maintaining the quality and personal attention we're known for.

  • Launched online classes
  • Developed custom learning platform
  • Reached students nationwide
2022

Global Classroom Launch

Unveiled our comprehensive digital academy with live masterclasses, on-demand video library, interactive puzzle trainer, and AI-powered game analysis. Our reach expanded internationally with students joining from USA, Canada, UAE, and across India.

  • Full-featured online portal
  • International student enrollment
  • AI-powered analysis tools
2024

Third Branch & Milestones

Opened our third Bikaner location in response to overwhelming demand. Celebrated producing our 100th tournament winner and crossing 1,000 active students. Introduced specialized programs for women's chess and chess for cognitive development in senior citizens.

  • 3rd branch inaugurated
  • 1000+ active students milestone
  • Launched specialized programs
2025

Leading Bikaner's Chess Revolution

Today, Perfect Chess Academy stands as Bikaner's premier chess institution with 1,250+ active students, 150+ tournament winners, and an alumni network of 5,000+ players. We continue innovating with AI-assisted training, international coaching collaborations, and community outreach programs.

  • 1,250+ active students
  • 150+ tournament champions
  • Pioneering AI-enhanced training
